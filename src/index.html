About Us

Many surprises to be expected at the WILD Wild Circus!

Wild Circus is a one-of-a-kind circus group produced by the Wild Entertainment Company, one of France’s most prominent live-shows producer since 1998. Some of the most acclaimed projects, such as The Eye of the Tiger, Let it go, let it go or Red Shoes gathered over a million spectators in 120 cities across the globe. The organization's creative approach includes a large variety of entertainment forms such as multimedia productions, immersive experiences and happenings involving the public. Expect to be mind-blown by our performers skills and sensitivity!
